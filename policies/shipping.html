Shipping information

Switzerland International

Domestic shipping

All domestic orders are shipped via Swiss Post with tracking.

International shipping

International orders are mostly shipped via DHL express unless the destination country cannot be serviced by DHL Express. In that case we ship via the international postal service (Swiss Post international).

Whenever available we use DHL Express and select the "Duties & Delivery Paid (DDP)" option which means that you, the recipient, won't be charged any import costs.

Below is a list of countries that DHL Express services but can't offer DDP for. If you live in one of these areas you will be charged import costs which are your sole responsibility.

If DHL Express does not services your country and we ship via postal service you will be charged import costs which are your sole responsibility.

In order to compensate for the additional import costs we offer all affected customers a 10% discount. Please contact support@shiftcrypto.ch before ordering to get the discount code.
