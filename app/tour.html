BitBoxApp tour

See how easy it is to use our app.

Transaction history

You can send or receive coins, check the balance and see the transaction history for:

  • Bitcoin
  • Litecoin
  • Ethereum (in beta)
  • Tether USD (in beta)
  • Chainlink (in beta)
  • Basic Attention Token (in beta)
  • Maker (in beta)
  • 0x (in beta)
  • Dai (in beta)

We recommend that you only store small amounts of Ethereum or tokens on it for testing purposes as their support is still in beta.

Ethereum and ERC20 tokens in the app are only supported for the BitBox02. If you want to use them with the BitBox01, you have to use the external website www.myetherwallet.com.

Receive coins

Get addresses to receive coins.

In case of the BitBox02, you can verify the address on your device. In case of BitBox01, we recommend that you use the mobile app to verify that the displayed address is correct.

Send coins

Create a transaction to spend your coins.

Confirm transaction

BitBox02

Confirm the transaction with the touch sliders on your device.

BitBox01

Hold the touch button to approve the transaction.

We recommend that you use the mobile app to verify that the transaction details are correct.

Settings

Manage your app settings here: Select which fiat currencies are relevant to you and which accounts you want to use, enable coin control or connect your own full node.

Manage device

BitBox02

Manage backups, change the device name, show the BIP39 mnemonic, enable the optional BIP39 passphrase or reset the device.

BitBox01

Manage backups, change the device password, create a hidden wallet, pair with the mobile app, enable 2FA or reset the device.
